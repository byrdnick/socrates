  • {% blocktrans trimmed %} Summary and Purpose of this Study The purpose of this study is to explore whether discussion helps people arrive at more accurate answers to tricky puzzles. Ultimately, we aim to develop tools that will help scientists better study people’s ordinary concepts and problem-solving capacities. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans trimmed %} Procedure In the course of this study, you will be asked to read one or more short passages on the computer and to answer questions about them. In addition, you may be asked to discuss your responses with another participant in an anonymous, real-time, text-only chat. The study proceeds in two stages. First, you will read a short passage and will answer questions about it. Second, you will be matched with another participant with whom you can communicate by text, remaining completely anonymous. After exchanging your opinions in a ‘chat’ window, we will ask you to re-evaluate your answers given in the ﬁrst stage. The total duration of study will be about {{ study_length }}. {% endblocktrans %}
